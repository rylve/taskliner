Last updated: July 21, 2026

Current release status Taskliner is a free, local-first browser app. Optional Google Drive sync is end-to-end encrypted. Optional Discord completion sharing posts from your browser to a Webhook you provide. Paid features are not available in the current release.

Using Taskliner

You may use Taskliner for lawful personal or professional task planning. You do not need an account or Google connection to use the local app. You are responsible for the content you enter and for keeping a JSON export or other backup when that content matters.

Your data

The current app stores your task data in your browser and does not provide a Taskliner-hosted task database. Optional Google Drive sync stores only end-to-end encrypted copies in your Google Drive appDataFolder. Browser storage can be cleared, corrupted, blocked, or lost by device and browser events. See the Data & Sync guide for the current recovery options.

If every approved device, usable Taskliner passkey, and recovery file for encrypted sync are lost, the Drive copy cannot be recovered by Taskliner, Google, or the operator. Keep an independent JSON export of important work.

Taskliner is provided as an evolving free service. We may change, pause, or discontinue features, including the public website, without guaranteeing uninterrupted availability. We will update these terms when a material change affects how the service or user data works.

Third-party services

Taskliner is delivered through Cloudflare Pages. The site may load Google Fonts, and Cloudflare Web Analytics may measure page views and basic performance. Those providers operate under their own terms and policies.

If you explicitly enable Discord completion sharing, your browser posts directly to the Discord Incoming Webhook you provide. Discord’s terms and privacy policy apply to that channel. Taskliner does not relay or store those posts on an operator server.

If you explicitly enable Google sync, Cloudflare Pages Functions handles the OAuth session and relays end-to-end encrypted artifacts to Google Drive using the drive.appdata scope, plus OpenID email for account identification. Taskliner does not request ordinary Drive files or operate a hosted database containing task content.
